Iced Tea


Refreshing and fruity iced tea for the summer!

Ingredients:

  • 4–6 tea bags (black, green, or herbal)
  • 4 cups (1 liter) hot water
  • 4 cups (1 liter) cold water
  • Ice
  • Sweetener (optional, e.g., sugar, honey, or syrup)
  • Lemon slices or mint (optional)

Method:

  1. Boil Water – Bring 4 cups of water to a near boil (about 190–200°F / 88–93°C).
  2. Steep the Tea – Add tea bags to the hot water and let them steep for 5–10 minutes (longer for stronger tea).
  3. Remove Tea Bags – Take out the tea bags and let the tea cool slightly.
  4. Sweeten (Optional) – Stir in sugar or honey while the tea is warm so it dissolves easily.
  5. Add Cold Water – Pour in 4 cups of cold water to dilute and cool the tea.
  6. Chill – Refrigerate for at least 1 hour or until cold.
  7. Serve Over Ice – Fill glasses with ice cubes and pour the chilled tea over.
  8. Garnish (Optional) – Add lemon slices, mint leaves, or fruit for extra flavor.
